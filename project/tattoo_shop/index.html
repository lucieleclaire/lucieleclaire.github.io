About

Where it all started

DFA Tattoos was founded in Montreal by a collective of driven and skilled artists with the goal of creating the best tattoos. From large scale work to quick walk-ins, we believe that every tattoo is important. We work in a variety of styles, from bold traditional to photo realism and portraits, we have an artist to suit all of your projects. We do our best to make sure our clients are comfortable, well-informed and safe. Our dedication to the craft pushes us every day to achieve results our clients can be proud to wear for a lifetime.
